About Us

Welcome to Kumar Guest House - a warm and inviting guest house located in the heart of Kharagpur. Our family-owned guest house has been offering comfortable and affordable accommodation to travelers and guests for over 10 years.

At Kumar Guest House, we take pride in providing our guests with a home away from home experience. Our friendly and professional staff is committed to ensuring that your stay with us is enjoyable and stress-free.

At Kumar Guest House, we are dedicated to providing our guests with a warm and welcoming atmosphere, comfortable accommodation, and exceptional service. We look forward to welcoming you to our guest house and helping you create unforgettable memories of your visit to Kharagpur.
